How to Play

Welcome to a journey of connection and discovery! This game contains 50 carefully crafted questions ranging from light-hearted fun to meaningful depth. Here's how to play:

  1. Take turns answering each question.
  2. Listen actively when your partner speaks.
  3. Be honest and open with your answers.
  4. Remember, there are no right or wrong answers.
  5. If a question feels too intense, feel free to skip it and come back later.
  6. Use the category filters to focus on specific types of questions.

The goal is to have fun while deepening your connection. Enjoy the journey together!
